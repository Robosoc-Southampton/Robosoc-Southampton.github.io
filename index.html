About us

RoboSoc is a hobbyist robotics society. We build robots from scratch for fun, whether it's for inter-university competitions, or just because we have a cool project idea to try out.

We welcome anyone with any level of experience, and will help you catch you up with the basics if you're new to robotics.

What we're up to this year

  1. Bunfights
  2. Online coding sessions
  3. New Committee
  4. SumoBot
